Chapter I — The Premise

A House That
Remembers Wrong

VR Horror Escape Room Narrative Single Player Meta Quest 3

You are Kamil — a young man trapped in the warped reflection of his childhood home, convinced his mother performed dark rituals that took his brother Faheem's life.

Hallways stretch. Objects shift. Faheem's ghost lingers in every reflection, every whisper. As your sanity drains, the house grows hungrier, and the puzzles grow stranger.

But the horrors here aren't waiting in the dark. They're waiting inside you. Schizo Shadows is a psychological horror escape room built for VR — where the real twist isn't the monster. It's the diagnosis.

Some shadows are memories.
Some memories never happened. — Kamil

Chapter III — Mechanics

The Anatomy of Fear

/ 01

Sanity System

A dynamic mental state that warps the environment in real time. The lower your sanity, the more the house bends — walls breathe, doors relocate, faces appear in the walls.

/ 02

VR Hallucinations

Faheem's ghost manifests through ScriptedGhostWalk patterns and SanityRevealedObjects — apparitions visible only when sanity dips below thresholds. Stare too long and pay the price.

/ 03

Environmental Puzzles

Ghost Footsteps. Shadow Handprint Trails. Pill Bottle Sorting. Every puzzle is built native-VR — designed for hand presence on Meta Quest 3 controllers, not adapted from flatscreen logic.

/ 04

Spatial Audio

FMOD-driven 3D sound design. Whispers come from behind you. Breathing tightens as sanity drops. The audio engineering carries the dread when the visuals go quiet.

/ 05

Branching Endings

Reality Acceptance. Denial. Neutral. The conclusion you reach depends on which puzzles you solve, how you treat the hallucinations, and whether Kamil ever trusts what he sees.

/ 06

Built for Quest 3

Unity 2022.3 LTS, XR Interaction Toolkit, optimized for standalone Meta Quest 3. No PC required. No tether. Step into Kamil's house, in his body, in your living room.
